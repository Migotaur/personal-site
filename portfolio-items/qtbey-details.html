Quest to beat El Yeehaw

Visual Novel

  • Started: January 2024
  • Status: In Development
  • Language(s): Python
  • Technologies used: Ren'Py, Microsoft Copilot (Image Gen), Paint.Net (Illustrations)

Quest to beat El Yeehaw is a visual novel currently in development. The story takes place within the Professional Wrestling industry. The user takes the role as the protagonist, where the end goal is to become the Undisputed Champion of the Pro Lucha Federation. The current champion and chairman of the Federation is a luchador that goes by the alias, El Yeehaw. Before the user can face El Yeehaw, however, they must work their way up through minor leagues and defeat weaker wrestlers. The user, alongside retired luchadora and manager Mox Mayberry, must work their way up to become the undisputed PLF champion.
